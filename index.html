About the Project {{name}}

This project is a collboration between the City of LA and Creative Labs at UCLA to celebrate Earth Day. It encourages the people of Los Angeles to keep LA thriving by showing them various initiatives by the city they can join. It also showcases some data collected by the LA City Open Data Initiative that shows the people how they can take a moment to enjoy the beautiful mother Earth. Hope you enjoy it!
